# Role Brief – Margin & Ops Analyst

## Mission
Keep the business profitable and predictable by maintaining live financial models, tracking operational KPIs, and managing policy levers (pricing, restock fee, inventory).

## Inputs Provided
- `financials.md` baseline model.
- Team plan + role outputs.
- Restock fee target: $30 (20% of base price).

## Key Deliverables
1. **Live Financial Model**
   - Includes device cost, labor assumptions, restock fee, return rate scenarios.
   - Auto-updated with real prep times + costs from tracker.
2. **Weekly KPI Dashboard**
   - Prep time, reset time, margin per unit, cash burn/runway, inventory on hand.
3. **Policy Documents**
   - Restocking policy wording (customer-facing + internal).
   - Reorder thresholds and purchasing schedule.
4. **Risk & Opportunity Brief**
   - Biweekly note highlighting margin threats, efficiency wins, pricing tweak proposals.

## Success Metrics
- Gross margin ≥70% maintained.
- Forecast variance within ±10%.
- Restock fee covers labor cost 100% of the time.

## Collaboration
- Pull data from Device Prep + Automation trackers.
- Provide Customer Writer with approved policy text.
- Alert Sales when inventory is low or order mix changes margin profile.

## Reporting Cadence
- Weekly dashboard post + commentary.
- Ad-hoc alerts for anomalies (return spike, cost change, etc.).
