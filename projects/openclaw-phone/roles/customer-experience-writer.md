# Role Brief – Customer Experience Writer

## Mission
Make the OpenClaw phone feel effortless for everyday users by owning every word they read, from the in-box insert to the landing page and concierge script.

## Inputs Provided
- Onboarding outline (`customer-onboarding.md`).
- Brand voice: direct, friendly, no jargon.
- Pricing tiers: $130 base / $180 with concierge.
- Restock fee: $30 (if like-new).

## Key Deliverables
1. **One-Page Insert (print-ready)**
   - Includes “What’s in the box,” 10-minute setup steps, QR to full guide, support contact.
2. **Full Onboarding Guide**
   - Detailed walkthrough with screenshots for API keys, channel hookup, first message.
3. **Landing Page Copy + FAQ**
   - Hero pitch, differentiation, pricing table, FAQ covering returns/restock, concierge, privacy.
4. **Concierge Call Script + Follow-Up Email**
   - Agenda + checklist for the $50 setup session.
   - Post-call email template summarizing actions + tips.

## Success Metrics
- Support ticket volume trends after launch (target <10% of customers needing help).
- Customer satisfaction survey results (8/10+).
- Time-to-first-successful message <15 minutes for new customers following the guide.

## Collaboration
- Sync with Support Lead on common issues to address in docs.
- Coordinate with Sales/Funnel for consistent messaging.
- Work with Ops Analyst to ensure policy language (returns, fees) matches financial model.

## Reporting Cadence
- Draft reviews shared within 48 hours of kickoff.
- Version log maintained for each asset.
