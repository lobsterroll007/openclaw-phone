# Role Brief – Sales & Funnel Builder

## Mission
Package the OpenClaw phone offer and drive early demand, ensuring every interested buyer flows through a clear intake, qualification, and payment process.

## Inputs Provided
- Offer structure: $130 base / $180 concierge add-on, $30 restock fee.
- Target audience: average consumers who want AI without setup hassles.
- Payment methods (initial): cash, Cash App, Venmo, Zelle.

## Key Deliverables
1. **Order Intake Form + CRM Sheet**
   - Collects customer info, payment status, shipping details.
   - Pipeline view (Lead → Qualified → Paid → In Prep → Shipped → Follow-up).
2. **Sales Scripts & Collateral**
   - DM/email script for pitching.
   - Short landing page/one-pager summary for outreach.
   - Social media + referral blurb.
3. **Lead Sourcing Plan**
   - Initial channels (friends/family, local groups, maker communities, etc.).
   - Metrics for conversion per channel.
4. **Payment & Confirmation Flow**
   - Instructions for accepted payment methods.
   - Automated confirmation template (email/SMS/Telegram) with next steps.

## Success Metrics
- Leads generated/week.
- Qualified-to-paid conversion rate (>40% target initially).
- Average time from payment to ship notification (<3 days once ops stable).

## Collaboration
- Coordinate with Ops Analyst for inventory availability.
- Sync with Customer Writer to keep messaging consistent.
- Loop in Support Lead for after-sales check-ins.

## Reporting Cadence
- Weekly funnel metrics report.
- Immediate alert when demand exceeds prep capacity.
