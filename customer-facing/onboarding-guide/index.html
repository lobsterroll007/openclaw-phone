    OpenClaw Phone Onboarding Guide

    Direct, friendly, and built for non-technical humans.

    Before you start: Have your Wi-Fi password, preferred AI API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.), and Telegram/Signal login handy. Keep the included charger connected so the phone stays powered during setup.
