    Payment & Confirmation Flow – OpenClaw Phone

    1. Customer Says "Yes"

    1. Confirm Offer Tier & Timeline
    2. Reply with summary: "Got it — locking you in for the $130 Base ($180 Concierge) package. Next available 2-hour prep block is {{date/time}}."
    3. If special device/color requested, mention $30 restock fee and confirm acceptance before proceeding.
    4. Update CRM
    5. Stage → Qualified
    6. Add tentative prep block in calendar + notes

    2. Send Payment Instructions

    Share payment block tailored to customer preference (copy/paste from below):

    Choose whichever is easiest — just include "OpenClaw Phone" in the note so we track it:
• Cash (local handoff) – pay at pickup/drop-off, you'll get a written + digital receipt
• Cash App – $openclawops
• Venmo – @OpenClawOps (toggle purchases on)
• Zelle – finance@openclaw.co

Total: ${amount} (includes restock fee if applicable). Once it's sent, screenshot or forward the confirmation and I'll mark your prep slot.
    • Reminder: Concierge tier requires intake form (apps/login instructions). Send link immediately after payment confirmation.

    3. Verify Payment

    1. Check incoming notification or screenshot
    2. Record transaction ID/memo in CRM Notes
    3. Stage → Paid, update Last Updated

    4. Send Confirmation Template

    Payment received ✅

You're booked for the {{date/time}} prep block (2 hours). Here's what happens next:
1. Complete intake form: {{link}}
2. We'll prep the device, QA it, and document settings
3. Handoff/ship ETA: {{handoff date}}

If anything changes, reply here — otherwise expect a confirmation with either pickup instructions or tracking as soon as it's shipped.
    • For concierge: add "We'll confirm any app credentials via secure note before we start."

    5. Prep & Handoff

    • Follow phone-prep checklist; keep actual work within 2-hour window.
    • Upon completion, send delivery message with proof (photos, tracking #, or pickup instructions) and move stage → Shipped.

    6. Post-Delivery Follow-up

    • 3 days after delivery: Stage → Follow-up
    • Send template:
    How's the OpenClaw Phone treating you? Anything to tweak?
If it's working great, mind dropping a quick testimonial or referring someone who needs the "OpenClaw brain without the setup headache"? I can reserve them a slot next week.
    • Log any referrals + schedule incentives.

    Notes & Safeguards

    • Never start prep without confirmed payment
    • If prep calendar is full, offer to waitlist or upsell concierge priority (if sustainable)
    • Maintain consistent timestamps in CRM to audit turnaround as we push prep time below 2 hours
